About Thimma Enterprise

A Trusted Partner for Food Manufacturers Across India

Thimma Enterprise is a specialised B2B food ingredient distribution and export company headquartered in India. With over two decades of industry experience, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality consistency, and deep expertise in sourcing and supplying food-grade ingredients.

We work exclusively with food manufacturers, processors, and producers — supplying both imported ingredients and domestically sourced dairy-based ingredients from certified Indian dairy manufacturers. Our supply chain spans all of India and extends internationally to Singapore and Malaysia.

Our Mission

To be the most reliable ingredient supply partner for food businesses — ensuring quality, consistency and competitive pricing at every step.

Our Vision

To expand our distribution network globally while strengthening India's role as a food ingredient sourcing hub.

Ingredients for Every Food Manufacturing Need

We source and supply food-grade ingredients — both imported and domestically produced — ensuring consistent quality and reliable availability.

Whey Protein Concentrate

High-quality WPC sourced from certified international principals. Ideal for sports nutrition, bakery, and functional food applications requiring consistent protein content.

▲ Imported

Xanthan Gum

Food-grade xanthan gum for use as a stabiliser, thickener, and emulsifier. Widely used in sauces, dressings, beverages, and gluten-free formulations.

▲ Imported

Monosodium Glutamate (MSG)

Premium food-grade MSG for flavour enhancement applications. Supplied in bulk to food manufacturers and seasoning blenders across India.

▲ Imported

Skimmed Milk Powder (SMP)

High-grade SMP sourced from reputed Indian dairy manufacturers. Low fat content, ideal for dairy processing, bakery, confectionery, and reconstituted milk products.

Whole Milk Powder (WMP)

Full-cream WMP with rich flavour profile from quality-certified Indian dairy plants. Suitable for chocolate, beverages, infant nutrition, and dairy blends.

✓ Indian Dairy

Whey Powder

Sweet whey powder derived from Indian dairy operations. Used in baked goods, confectionery, and processed food as a versatile nutritional ingredient.
